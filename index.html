3. Information we collect

When you install and use the App, we may collect and process:

3.2 Information about orders and customers

To provide AI-channel analytics and attribution, we access certain data about your orders and customers from Shopify, including:

We use this data only to provide and improve the Service (for example, to detect and label orders that likely came from AI assistants such as ChatGPT, Perplexity, Gemini, or Microsoft Copilot).
